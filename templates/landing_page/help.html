How to Use Habitize

1. Account Management

Create an account or log in to get started with Habitize.
If you already have a Google account, you can also log in using it:

- Click the "Log in with Google" button on the login page.
- Follow the prompts to log in using your Google credentials.
- You're all set to use Habitize with your Google account.

2. Adding Habits

Follow these steps to add a new habit:

- Choose a category for your habit.
- Provide a habit name.
- Set a goal for your habit.
- Specify the unit of measurement.
- Select the frequency for your habit (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly).

3. Editing Habits

You can edit your habits by following these steps:

- Click the three dots on the right side of the habit.
- Adjust the current amount to track your habit's progress.

4. Completing and Deleting Habits

You can complete or delete a habit by clicking the three dots next to the habit entry.

5. Filtering by Frequency

In the habit section of the dashboard, you can filter your habits by frequency (Daily, Weekly, or Monthly).

6. Tracking Habit Progress

You can monitor the progress of your habits using:

- The number on the left side of the habit entry.
- The progress bar behind the habit.

7. Viewing Completed Habits

Check your progress in completing habits in the "Completed Habits" section of the dashboard.
