How to use Habitize

1. Create an account or login.

2. Add your first habit by clicking the add button.
- When adding a habit pic a category for your habit.
- Add a habit name.
- Pick a habit name.
- set a goal of your choice.
- Set your unit of measurement.
- Choose a frequency for your habit i.e. Daily, Weekly or Monthly.

3. You can edit your habit by clicking the three dots on the right side of the habit.
- When editing your habit you can adjust your current amount to progress your habit.

4. You can also complete or delete your habit by clicking the three dots.

5.In the habit section of the dashboard you can filter your habits by frequency.

5. You can monitor the progress of your habit by the number on the left side of the habit and the bar behind the habit.

6. You can also see your progress of completing your habits in the completed habits section of the dashboard.
